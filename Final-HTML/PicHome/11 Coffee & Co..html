Hello!
Welcome to 11 Coffee & Co. We are a brand new neighbourhood coffee shop in Ealing with a difference!

We offer amazing food, nothing short of labour of love, with fantastic coffee together with a rotation of single estate guest coffee, served by friendly team in a relaxed and cosy environment.

Come and experience the difference!

Our mission and philosophy is simple at 11 Coffee & Co - to serve great food and excellent coffee in a welcoming and friendly atmosphere.

Since we opened our door in December 2015, we have become a favourite and a go-to destination in the neighbourhood for delicious breakfast, brunch and savoury dishes as well as award winning cakes and beautifully made coffee.

11 Coffee & Co is the fruit of 11 years of experience having opened our first branch, Piacere, in 2005 in Hampstead Garden Suburbs of London.

Our passion and love of home-cooked meal is portrayed in every dish that is served.
