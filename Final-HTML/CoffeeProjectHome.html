CAN COFFEE


Coffee Times opened in Lexington, KY in 1983 before the American coffee shop experience had fully developed.
It was an exciting time that afforded us the opportunity to grow and develop while staying true to our mission of providing freshly roasted 100% Arabica coffee with great service and personality.

Driven by our passion for coffee, we have progressed into a coffee house with offerings that include single origin coffees, fair-trade organic coffees and exceptional flavored coffees, all roasted and flavored on site. We offer an extensive loose leaf tea selection, Cocoas, and brewing devices along-side a fun collection of goods & things. At our Espresso Bar you’ll enjoy our daily brews, specialty drink options, fresh baked goods and a light lunch menu.
